Loku gin is a premium craft gin that combines a blend of unique botanicals to create a distinctive and refreshing taste. Its botanicals include hand-picked juniper berries, coriander seeds, angelica root, and lemon peel. These botanicals are carefully balanced to create a smooth, sophisticated flavor that is perfect for sipping or mixing in cocktails. The gin has a subtle citrus flavor, with hints of spice and a refreshing finish. Loku gin is distilled in small batches, using traditional methods to ensure the highest quality and flavor. It is perfect for gin enthusiasts who appreciate a premium, handcrafted spirit that is bursting with character and flavor.
